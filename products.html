VetBot

Introducing VetBot, an innovative product designed to revolutionize the vetting and protocol of radiological scans. Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and large language models (LLMs), VetBot ensures precise and efficient analysis of radiological data. This advanced tool streamlines the workflow for radiologists, reducing the time and effort required to vet scans while maintaining high accuracy. VetBot's AI-driven technology enhances diagnostic capabilities, supports clinical decision-making, and ultimately improves patient outcomes in radiology. Currently, VetBot is being tested at Guy's and St Thomas' NHS Trust, where it is demonstrating its potential to transform radiological practices within a leading healthcare institution.

AppMate

Introducing our revolutionary app that harnesses AI and natural voice technology to streamline radiology appointments. Our app can simultaneously call up to 5 patients, efficiently booking them for their scans. This innovative solution not only saves significant time and resources but also minimizes the waste associated with missed appointments. Experience the future of radiology scheduling with our cutting-edge app, designed to enhance efficiency and ensure no patient appointment goes unfilled.
